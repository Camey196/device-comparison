Xbox

Xbox is a video gaming brand created and owned by Microsoft. It represents a series of video game consoles developed by Microsoft, with consoles in the sixth to ninth generations. The brand also represents applications (games), streaming services, an online service by the name of Xbox network, and the development arm by the name of Xbox Game Studios.

  • Launch Year: 2001
  • Latest Console: Xbox Series X
  • Popular Games: Halo, Gears of War, Forza Horizon

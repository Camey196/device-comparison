PlayStation

PlayStation is a video gaming brand created and owned by Sony Interactive Entertainment. It represents a series of video game consoles developed by Sony, with consoles in the fifth to ninth generations. The brand also represents applications (games), streaming services, an online service by the name of PlayStation Network, and the development arm by the name of PlayStation Studios.

  • Launch Year: 1994
  • Latest Console: PlayStation 5
  • Popular Games: God of War, The Last of Us, Uncharted
